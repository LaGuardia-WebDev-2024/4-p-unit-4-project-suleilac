SuliDrawz

On the 14th of May in 2020, a small creator SuliDrawz rose into the storytime animation community. With her iconic purple beanie defining her from the crowd, this young aspiring artist took advantage of staying home due to quarantine and spent her time working.

Inspired by famous content creators such as JaidenAnimations, SomethingElseYt and TheOdd1sOut, she was determined to create her own spin on telling stories about her life.

Since she started, she used her platform to upload quick funny animated tiktoks, make short storytime videos and post drawings that she made for fun. She was able to collaborate with other artists in a welcoming community where she had a platform to influence, grow and be productive with her time home!

She was even hired to animate a program's logo, and draw a book cover for them!
